# Privacy Policy for Bible Puzzle Quiz

**Effective Date:** February 19, 2026

Bible Puzzle Quiz ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
Depending on how you use the app, we may collect the following information:
- **Guest Access:** If you play as a guest, we only collect a display name of your choice.
- **Email Login:** If you register with an email, we collect your email address and a password.
- **Google Login:** If you sign in using Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google.

### 1.2 Game Data and Progress
We collect data related to your interaction with the app to provide a personalized experience:
- Puzzle completion status and progress.
- "Faith Duel" (challenge) history and statistics.
- Sponsorship or support status.

### 1.3 Device and Usage Information
We may collect technical information such as:
- Browser type and version.
- Operating system.
- Interaction data (e.g., time spent on specific puzzles).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- **To provide and maintain the service:** Saving your puzzle progress and allowing you to resume play.
- **To facilitate social features:** Enabling "Faith Duels" where you can challenge friends via email or shared links.
- **To personalize your experience:** Displaying your username and avatar.
- **To process support/donations:** Managing sponsorship status through third-party payment processors (e.g., Google Play Billing).
- **To improve our app:** Analyzing usage patterns to enhance gameplay and content.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage:** We use your browser's local storage to save your game progress locally on your device.
- **Security:** We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Google Authentication:** For secure sign-in.
- **Google Play Billing:** For processing voluntary sponsorships and donations.
- **Gemini API:** We may use AI services to enhance content or provide interactive features.

These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

## 5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:
- **With your consent:** For example, when you send a challenge link to a friend.
- **For legal reasons:** If required by law or to protect our rights.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

- **Retention:** We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.
- **Deletion:** You can request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time. To do so, please contact us at the email address provided below. You can also clear your local game progress by clearing your browser's local storage.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Bible Puzzle Quiz is a family-friendly app. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without such consent, we will take steps to delete that information from our servers.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
